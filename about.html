ONLINE STORE OF CANNABIS SEEDS ERRORS-SEEDS IS UKRAINIAN SYDBANK

We have collected the best varieties from world famous manufacturers. All the seeds presented in the store are a F2 generation and are obtained in Indore under conditions as close to ideal as possible. As a result, our seeds are in no way inferior to F1 seeds, which are sold in foreign online stores known sid banks. We send seeds to all points of the world.

When ordering more than 10 seeds, you get a bonus of 20% (there are 2 bonus seeds for every 10 purchased.) The bonus applies only to the seeds of our production. Foreign manufacturers go without bonuses). All our seeds are regular. The minimum order in our store is 3 seeds (or for the amount of 100grn)..

Despite the fact that the purchase and sale of cannabis seeds are completely legal in Ukraine operations, parcels are sent in inconspicuous packaging, or by registered mail, without specifying the contents of the package.

All information provided on the site is for informational purposes only. By being on the site you confirm that you are 18 years old.

The store administration recalls that the hemp seeds are in free circulation on the territory of Ukraine and CIS countries. We sell cannabis seeds as food for parrots, fish, souvenirs and for scientific research. For any illegal actions on the part of customers, the store does not bear responsibility. We strongly recommend not using cannabis seeds for other purposes than those listed above.
